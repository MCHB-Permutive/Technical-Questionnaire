What we'll ask, and why

Nothing here is busywork — each answer shapes how quickly and smoothly we can get you live.

Your team

So we reach the right technical contacts from day one and plan around how your engineering team actually ships — rather than chasing the wrong people mid-rollout.

Timeline & platforms

Knowing your deadline and which surfaces are in scope — web, AMP, iOS, Android, CTV — lets us sequence the work sensibly and give you a realistic go-live plan instead of a guess.

Your ad stack

Your ad server and demand connections (Prebid, AdX, direct, your SSPs and DSPs) tell us exactly where Permutive plugs in, so targeting and reporting work from the start.

Identity & data

The identifiers you can share let us recognise your audiences across browsers, devices and domains — and, if you route data, we make sure it lands in the right warehouse.

Domains, apps & CTV

A clear list of every site and app is what we scope the integration against, so nothing is missed and each property is accounted for in the deployment.

A note on your data. This is a static page — there's no server collecting your answers in the background. Everything you enter lives only inside the link you generate, which stays in your hands until you choose to send it to us. Please share that link only with people you trust, as anyone who opens it can see the responses.
